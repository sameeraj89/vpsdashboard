💡 The 12-Month Thesis
LHRC is a debt-free ₹452 Cr hospital at 21.8% EBITDA — positioned at the inflection point of a landmark transformation. The 12-month plan runs in three distinct phases: Phase 1 (Apr–May) locks in quick-win cost saves and structural decisions; Phase 2 (Jun–Sep) executes HR rationalisation, procurement reform, and digital infrastructure; Phase 3 (Oct–Mar) captures the full revenue upside via oncology ramp, spine robotics, occupancy expansion and payor mix optimisation. The ₹50 Cr EBITDA uplift above budget is achievable by executing both the revenue engine (ARPOB ₹35K→₹41K, occupancy 53%→68%) and the cost lever (staffing ₹13 Cr + procurement ₹5 Cr + energy ₹1 Cr + doctor fee ₹1 Cr) simultaneously.
