⬦ Why South Asian Population Genetics Matters to Pharma
South Asian patients show distinct mutation frequencies (higher EGFR exon 19/21 in lung, BRCA founder variants, higher PIK3CA, lower KRAS) vs Western populations. Indian patients are systematically under-represented in global clinical trials, creating a gap that CDSCO now requires bridging for drug approvals. This cohort is one of the only South Asian datasets with matched clinical + genomic + outcome data — a unique asset for global pharma label expansion studies.

COSMIC v3.4 mutational signatures decomposed from WES/WGS data (n=312). Each signature reflects an aetiological process — tobacco, APOBEC, HRD, MMRD, ageing. These are directly used by pharma to identify patients likely to respond to PARP inhibitors, IO, or specific chemotherapy.
SBS1 + SBS5 — Ageing
Spontaneous deamination of 5-methylcytosine + unknown replication error. Universal, increases with age.
72% of cases · Dominant in Haemato
SBS2 + SBS13 — APOBEC
APOBEC3A/3B cytidine deaminase activity. Strong predictor of IO response in breast + cervical cancers.
48% of cases · Strong in Breast/Cervical
SBS3 — HRD / BRCA
Homologous recombination deficiency. Predicts PARP inhibitor and platinum sensitivity. Key pharma biomarker.
24% of cases · Breast TNBC enriched
SBS4 + SBS29 — Tobacco + Areca Nut
SBS4 = tobacco carcinogens. SBS29 = areca nut-specific — unique South Asian signature. Highest globally in this cohort.
61% of Oral/H&N · Highest globally
SBS6 / SBS15 — MMRD
Mismatch repair deficiency. Associated with MSI-H status — predicts pembrolizumab pan-tumour eligibility.
18% of Haemato · MSI-H concordant
SBS35 — Prior Platinum
Platinum-induced damage signature. Identifies patients with prior platinum exposure — treatment history validation.
14% of cases · Post-chemotherapy
ctDNA / Liquid Biopsy — 248 patients longitudinally tracked
Serial ctDNA profiling enables minimal residual disease (MRD) detection, resistance mutation tracking (e.g., ESR1 in breast, EGFR T790M), and treatment response monitoring without repeat biopsy. This longitudinal liquid biopsy dataset is a key asset for pharma CDx development.
