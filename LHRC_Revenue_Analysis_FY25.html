🚨 Budget Shortfall Analysis
FY25 achieved only 85.3% of ₹480 Cr revenue budget. The gap widened from H2: Oct 84.5%, Nov 82.3%, Dec 84.2%, Feb 71.7% (worst month). IP discharges at 82.3% (21,023 vs 25,555) is the primary driver — occupancy averaged 52% vs 63% budgeted. The budget assumed aggressive ramp-up that didn't materialize. OP visits at 90.3% performed better, suggesting demand exists but IP conversion is the bottleneck.
