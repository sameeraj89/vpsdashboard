Other Notes
UNA Strike — Situation and Response
The United Nurses Association (UNA) called a strike affecting nursing staff across Kerala hospitals, including Lakeshore. The immediate impact has been felt on routine ward staffing and OT scheduling. Lakeshore has activated its contingency staffing protocol: agency nursing has been deployed to critical care and ICU, senior nurses have been redeployed to higher-acuity areas, and elective procedures have been rescheduled to minimise patient impact. Emergency and critical services have remained fully operational throughout.
Discussions with UNA leadership are ongoing. Management has engaged a mediator and a resolution framework covering the key demands — pay revision, duty hour norms, and leave entitlements — is being drafted for presentation to both parties. The administration's position is to resolve this through structured negotiation rather than contractual displacement. A resolution is expected to be tabled by end of the week. In the interim, patient safety remains the non-negotiable priority and all critical rosters are covered.
