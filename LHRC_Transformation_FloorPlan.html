⚡ PHASE 1 — FY27 H2 (Oct 2027 – Mar 2028)
Foundation Floors: Radiation Vault, Cancer ICU/BMT, Day Care, Head & Neck OPD  |  Est. ₹50–65 Cr
  • B1 — LINAC Vault & Radiation Bunker: Radiation-shielded civil construction, medical physics lab, HDR brachytherapy vault. Most critical long-lead item — AERB bunker approval required ~6 months before works start. PET-CT pit construction concurrent.₹35–45 Cr
  • GF — Dedicated Cancer Reception, Screening & Chemo Day Care: Segregated cancer entry separate from general hospital. 30-chair infusion centre with individual entertainment, genetic risk counseling desk, integrated oncology pharmacy, Gulf Corridor welcome lounge.₹4–6 Cr
  • 1F — Cancer ICU, BMT Unit & Head & Neck OPD: HEPA-filtered BMT rooms (8 beds with laminar flow isolation), Cancer ICU (16 beds). Head & Neck OPD: dedicated H&N surgery consultations, voice & swallowing lab, flexible nasoendoscopy suite, microvascular MDT room. First dedicated H&N centre in Kerala.₹10–14 Cr
🚀 PHASE 2 — FY28 H1 (Apr 2028 – Sep 2028)
Upper Floors: OTs, Clinical Trials, Radiation, Genomics, Rare Disease, IPD Wards  |  Est. ₹65–90 Cr
  • 2F — Surgical Oncology OTs & Head & Neck Theatre: 6 cancer-dedicated OTs including robotic surgery platform (Da Vinci: prostatectomy, rectal, gastric), 1 dedicated H&N/microvascular OT with intraoperative nerve monitoring (NIM), oncology CSSD with cancer-specific sterility protocols.₹18–24 Cr
  • 3F — Medical Oncology OPD & Clinical Trials Unit: Outpatient consultations, Phase I–III clinical trial unit with study coordinators, dedicated oncology pharmacy. Academic partnership with Amrita IMS for trial enrolment and research publications.₹4–6 Cr
  • 4F — Radiation Oncology & Brachytherapy: CT-simulator, treatment planning workstations, external beam bays (IMRT/SBRT/SRS/IGRT), HDR brachytherapy suite. LINAC commissioning target FY28 Q1. Provision for third LINAC in B1 by FY29 based on demand.₹12–16 Cr
  • 5F — Precision Medicine, Genomics & Rare Disease Centre: NGS sequencing, liquid biopsy, ctDNA profiling, OncoHelix tie-up. Rare Disease beds (12): thalassemia, SMA, Gaucher/Fabry, metabolic disorders. Genetic counseling, carrier screening, prenatal diagnostics. Tumor board suite. First such integrated centre in Kerala — core CarT differentiation.₹14–20 Cr
  • 6F–8F — Oncology IPD Wards & Palliative Care: Surgical oncology (38 beds incl. H&N post-op with FLAP monitoring, tracheostomy care), medical oncology (36 beds, laminar flow isolation, immunotherapy suites), palliative & rehabilitation floor (18 beds, psycho-oncology, physio gym).₹10–16 Cr
