⚠ Sequencing Risk — GF Congestion

Four separate Ground Floor projects (Central Cutout ₹34L, Ortho OPD ₹1.60 Cr, Casualty ₹3.80 Cr, Cutout Roof ₹40L) are running near-simultaneously. The future-state plan requires this same GF to become the Dedicated Cancer Reception & 30-Chair Chemo Day Care in FY27 H2. These active works must be sequenced as precursors to the transformation, not concurrent with it. A defined hand-over milestone is needed before FY27 H2 transformation works begin.

✓ Infrastructure Workstreams — Parallel to Transformation

MLCP (₹4.41 Cr, machinery in production) and STP Renovation (₹37L, tender submitted) are utility-layer projects that run parallel to, not as blockers of, the transformation. Both must be tracked as separate workstreams in the master programme. Parking area leveling (₹35L) is scheduled post-MLCP installation — already correctly sequenced.
